# RFQ – 1-Cell Li-ion Polymer Battery with NTC and PCM

**Use:** BLE wearable controller (professional healthcare environment)  
**Quantities:** EVT/DVT small lots, then ramp

## Electrical / Mechanical
- 3.7 V (4.2 V max), 300–500 mAh pouch, ≤3.5 mm thick preferred
- PCM integrated (OVP/UVP/OCP/short)
- Third-wire NTC: 10 k @25 °C, B≈3435 (bonded to can)
- Connector: JST-GH 3-pin; pin1=VBAT+, pin2=GND, pin3=NTC
- Cable 50–150 mm, strain relief

## Compliance (attach reports)
- IEC 62133-2, UN 38.3; UL 2054 preferred
- MSDS, RoHS, REACH, CoC; lot traceability
