# Incoming Inspection – Battery Packs

- Verify label: voltage, capacity, lot, date
- Check pinout vs. JST-GH: 1=VBAT, 2=GND, 3=NTC
- Measure open-circuit voltage: 3.7–3.9 V typical on receipt
- Measure NTC at 25 °C: ≈10 kΩ (±5%)
- Visual: pouch damage, leads strain relief, insulation
- Documentation present: IEC 62133-2, UN 38.3, UL 2054/MSDS
